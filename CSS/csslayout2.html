Maserati S.p.A. is an Italian luxury vehicle manufacturer.
Established on 1 December 1914, in Bologna, Italy, the company's
headquarters are now in Modena, and its emblem is a trident.
The company has been owned by Stellantis since 2021.
Maserati was initially associated with Ferrari
